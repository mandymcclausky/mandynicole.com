M2 design, code, and stuff

Mandy is a multi-disciplinary designer based in beautiful Portland, OR.

I've been building things on the web for 15 years. I love every aspect of the process — from wireframing and building prototypes based on UX maps, to experimenting with new technologies and running user tests.

While I prefer to design in the browser whenever possible, I'm exceptionally versed in Photoshop and Illustrator and have several years of experience working in print as well.
